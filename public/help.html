How to Use The Vault

Searching for Games

Navigate to the Home page and type the name of any video game (e.g., "Halo", "Minecraft", or "Elden Ring") into the search bar. Press the Search button or hit Enter to pull up the matching titles from our database.

Saving to Your Vault

Once you find a game you are interested in playing later, simply click the 🤍 Save button located at the bottom of the game's card. This will eventually send the game's information to your personal Supabase database so you never lose track of your backlog!
