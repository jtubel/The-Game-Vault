About The Project

The Game Vault was developed as a final project for INST377 at the University of Maryland, College Park.

The main problem this application solves is the choice dilemma that gamers face today. With thousands of games across multiple storefronts, it can be overwhelming to find new titles. This clean, intuitive interface strips away the ads and complex menus to provide just the essential information.

Our Data

All game data, including covers, release dates, and community ratings, is proudly powered by the RAWG Video Games Database API.
